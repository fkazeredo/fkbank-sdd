# Getting started with FKBANK

This guide covers what you can do in FKBANK today: open an account, sign in, and see your account
area. The banking features themselves arrive in later releases, and this page grows with them.

## Opening an account

1. Open FKBANK in your browser. You land on the welcome page.
2. Choose **Create account**.
3. Fill in your full name, CPF, e-mail address, a password, your date of birth and the income you
   declare each month.
4. Confirm. FKBANK checks your details with a credit bureau and tells you the result.

A few things worth knowing before you start:

- **Enter your full name — first and family name.** A single word is not enough; the bureau
  matches your whole legal name. The form tells you before you submit if something is missing.
- **Your CPF can be typed either way.** `123.456.789-09` and `12345678909` are the same number to
  us. It is checked as you type, so a mistyped digit is caught before you submit.
- **Your e-mail address is what you will sign in with.** It has to be one no other FKBANK account
  already uses.
- **Your password needs at least eight characters, including a letter and a digit.**
- **You have to be 18 or older.** If you turn 18 today, today counts.
- **The income you declare is a figure you tell us, not one we verify.** It is used later to
  decide the transaction limits you are offered. It is never a balance and never money you hold.

### What can happen next

| Result | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Approved** | The bureau confirmed your details. Your account is open with a balance of R$ 0,00. | Sign in with your e-mail address and password. |
| **Being checked** | The bureau has not answered yet. Your application stays open. | Wait — the answer arrives on its own. Come back to the page, or submit the same CPF again, to see where it got to. |
| **Not approved** | The bureau did not confirm your details. No account was opened. | The page shows the category behind the decision. If it points at something you can correct, correct it and apply again. |
| **You already have an account** | That CPF or e-mail address already belongs to an FKBANK account. | Sign in instead. If you do not recognize the account, contact support. |

**Submitting twice is safe.** If you press the button again, come back to the form later, or your
connection drops part-way, FKBANK shows you the application you already have rather than starting
a second one. One CPF never produces two accounts.

Your application has an address of its own. Keep the page open, or keep the link, and you can
check the outcome without signing in — which matters, because you cannot sign in until the
application succeeds.

## Signing in

1. Choose **Sign in** on the welcome page.
2. Enter the e-mail address and password you signed up with.
3. You are returned to FKBANK, signed in.

Sign-in details only work once your application has been approved. Until then they do not exist as
far as signing in is concerned, even though you chose the password when you applied.

## What you see after signing in

Your account area shows what your account holds — R$ 0,00 to begin with, because nothing has
arrived yet — along with your branch and account number. The branch is `0001`; the account number
is yours and does not change.

The top bar shows the FKBANK name, the account you are signed in as, and a **Sign out** button.
Below it sits the product navigation with six areas:

| Area | What it will hold |
|---|---|
| **Account** | Your balance, statement and receipts |
| **PIX** | PIX keys, transfers, refunds and QR codes |
| **Pay** | Paying boletos and topping up your account |
| **Boxes** | Yield boxes for money you want to set aside |
| **Card** | Your virtual debit card |
| **Credit** | Personal loan simulation and contracting |

Areas other than Account currently show a short placeholder. That is expected: the navigation
exists from the first release so the shape of the product is visible, and each area opens for real
when its feature ships.

You can move through the whole application with the keyboard: `Tab` moves between fields and
navigation entries, and `Enter` confirms or opens the highlighted one.

## Signing out

Select **Sign out** in the top bar. Your session is discarded immediately and you return to the
welcome page. FKBANK does not keep your session on the device after you sign out, and closing the
browser tab ends it as well.

## If something goes wrong

**"We could not complete your sign-in."** — the sign-in response did not match the request your
browser started. This usually means the page sat open for a long time, or sign-in was opened in
one tab and finished in another. Return to FKBANK and sign in again from the start.

**Your application is still being checked after a long time** — that is the bureau taking its
time, not something you did. Your application stays open and the answer applies when it arrives.
Submitting the same CPF again just shows you the same application.

**Sign-in is refused with details you are sure are right** — check that your application was
approved. Sign-in details do not work while an application is still being checked, and they never
work if it was refused.

**You are sent back to the sign-in page repeatedly** — your session has expired. Sign in again.

**A screen shows nothing at all** — your session may have ended while the page was open. Reload
the page; if it persists, sign out and back in.
