# Getting started with FKBANK

This guide covers what you can do in FKBANK today: sign in and reach your account area. The
banking features themselves arrive in later releases, and this page grows with them.

## Signing in

1. Open FKBANK in your browser.
2. You are taken to the sign-in page automatically — FKBANK never shows an area of the
   application before knowing who you are.
3. Enter your username and password, then confirm.
4. You are returned to FKBANK, signed in.

While your account is being created for you by the team (self-service sign-up is not available
yet), a pilot credential is provided to you separately. It only works on pilot environments.

## What you see after signing in

The top bar shows the FKBANK name, the account you are signed in as, and a **Sign out** button.
Below it sits the product navigation with six areas:

| Area | What it will hold |
|---|---|
| **Account** | Your balance, statement and receipts |
| **PIX** | PIX keys, transfers, refunds and QR codes |
| **Pay** | Paying boletos and topping up your account |
| **Boxes** | Yield boxes for money you want to set aside |
| **Card** | Your virtual debit card |
| **Credit** | Personal loan simulation and contracting |

Each area currently shows a short placeholder. That is expected: the navigation exists from the
first release so the shape of the product is visible, and each area opens for real when its
feature ships.

You can move through the whole application with the keyboard: `Tab` moves between navigation
entries and `Enter` opens the highlighted one.

## Signing out

Select **Sign out** in the top bar. Your session is discarded immediately and you return to the
sign-in page. FKBANK does not keep your session on the device after you sign out, and closing
the browser tab ends it as well.

## If something goes wrong

**"We could not complete your sign-in."** — the sign-in response did not match the request your
browser started. This usually means the page sat open for a long time, or sign-in was opened in
one tab and finished in another. Return to FKBANK and sign in again from the start.

**You are sent back to the sign-in page repeatedly** — your session has expired. Sign in again.

**A screen shows nothing at all** — your session may have ended while the page was open. Reload
the page; if it persists, sign out and back in.
